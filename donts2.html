Pregnancy Do's and Don'ts: Second Trimester

Action Do Don't
Continue eating a balanced diet Include plenty of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins Avoid excessive caffeine and limit intake of high-mercury fish
Stay hydrated Drink plenty of water throughout the day Avoid sugary beverages and excessive caffeine
Engage in regular exercise Continue with low-impact exercises like walking, swimming, or prenatal yoga Avoid high-impact activities and contact sports
Get plenty of rest Listen to your body and prioritize sleep Avoid lying flat on your back for extended periods, especially in the later stages of pregnancy
High-risk activities Discuss any potential risks with your healthcare provider before engaging in activities like scuba diving or hot yoga Avoid activities with a high risk of falling or abdominal injury


Note: Please for more Information and Details consult with your doctor

WELCOME TO THE SECOND TRIMESTER OF YOUR PREGNANCY

Second Trimester: Changes in Mother's Body

During the second trimester of pregnancy, the mother's body experiences various changes as the baby continues to grow and develop:

  • Fetal Movement: Women often start feeling the baby's movements, known as quickening, during the second trimester.
  • Growing Belly: The uterus expands, causing the belly to visibly grow larger.
  • Skin Changes: Some women may develop stretch marks on the abdomen, breasts, hips, and thighs as the skin stretches to accommodate the growing baby.
  • Decreased Nausea: Many women experience relief from morning sickness and nausea during the second trimester.
  • Increased Energy: Women often feel more energetic and less fatigued compared to the first trimester.
  • Backaches and Round Ligament Pain: As the belly grows, women may experience backaches and discomfort from stretching ligaments.

It's important for expectant mothers to continue with regular prenatal care, eat a balanced diet, and stay physically active during the second trimester to support their health and the health of their growing baby.

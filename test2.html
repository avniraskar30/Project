Tests required during the second trimester of pregnancy:

  • Blood glucose screening:

    Checks for gestational diabetes.

  • AFP screening (Alpha-fetoprotein):

    Detects neural tube defects and chromosomal abnormalities.

  • Ultrasound for fetal anatomy:

    Examines the baby's organs and development.

  • Quad screen:

    Checks for Down syndrome, trisomy 18, and neural tube defects.

  • Cell-free fetal DNA testing (cffDNA):

    Non-invasive prenatal screening for chromosomal abnormalities.

  • Amniocentesis:

    Diagnostic test for genetic abnormalities and fetal lung maturity.

  • Gestational diabetes test:

    Confirms gestational diabetes diagnosis.

  • Group B strep (GBS) screening:

    Detects bacteria that can be harmful to the baby during childbirth.

  • Rhogam injection (if Rh negative):

    Prevents Rh incompatibility complications.



Note: Please for more Information and Details consult with your doctor

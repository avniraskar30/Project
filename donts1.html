Pregnancy Do's and Don'ts: First Trimester

Action Do Don't
Eat a balanced diet Include plenty of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins Avoid raw or undercooked meat, fish, and eggs
Stay hydrated Drink plenty of water throughout the day Avoid excessive caffeine
Take prenatal vitamins Follow your healthcare provider's recommendations for prenatal supplements Avoid self-medicating without consulting your healthcare provider
Exercise regularly Engage in low-impact exercises such as walking, swimming, or prenatal yoga Avoid high-impact activities and contact sports
Use certain medications Consult your healthcare provider before taking any medications Avoid medications known to be harmful during pregnancy unless prescribed by your healthcare provider
Engage in strenuous activities Take breaks and avoid overexertion Avoid lifting heavy objects or engaging in activities that strain the abdomen
